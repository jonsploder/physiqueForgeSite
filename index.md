# PhysiqueForge

Welcome to the official site for PhysiqueForge, your mobile-first gym tracker.

PhysiqueForge follows a strict privacy-first philosophy: all your workout logs, history, and personal settings are stored locally on your device. We do not collect or have access to your data.
